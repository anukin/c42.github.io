rubymonk
rubymonk is an online training platform that teaches you ruby in a fun interactive way. It is designed to cater to complete newcomers to the programming world, as well as professionals looking for specific learning.
With our public beta wildly successful we're looking to make rubymonk the first choice for learning ruby.
The rubymonk Primer is a free introductory course on Ruby. It is our way of giving back to the community. We love ruby and we want you to know why
